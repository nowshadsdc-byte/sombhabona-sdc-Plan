**Joint Initiative: One Donation, Change Two Lives**

These two organizations have come together to launch a meaningful social impact initiative called **"One Donation, Change Two Lives."**

With a single donation of **USD 250 (approximately BDT 30,000)**, a donor can transform the lives of **two people**:

* **One Child** receives **one year of quality education**.  
* **One Woman** receives **a 3-month Caregiver Training Program**, equipping her with professional skills to secure sustainable employment.

The project will be fully managed and implemented by **Sombhabona**, ensuring that every donation is used effectively to create measurable and lasting impact.

### **Complete Transparency**

Transparency is at the heart of this initiative. Every donor will be able to see exactly how their contribution is making a difference.

The platform will include the following features:

* A public list of all supported **children and women** participating in the program.  
* Donors can browse and **select the specific child and woman** they wish to support.  
* Donor can pay online , Offline   
* Each beneficiary will have a detailed profile with their background and progress.  
* Every donor will have a secure login account to track updates, milestones, and success stories of the beneficiaries they support.  
* Donors will be able to view the community of supporters by accessing a list of all project donors, promoting openness and accountability.  
* Regular progress reports, photos, and status updates will be shared throughout the program so donors can witness the real impact of their contribution.
